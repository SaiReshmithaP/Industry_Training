Cultural Highlights

Visakhapatnam is rich in traditions, festivals, and art. The city celebrates Ugadi, Pongal, and other regional festivals with great enthusiasm.

Delicious Cuisine

Some popular dishes include:

  • Pulihora (Tamarind Rice)
  • Andhra-style Biryani
  • Chepala Pulusu (Fish Curry)
