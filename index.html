About Visakhapatnam

Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is a beautiful coastal city located in Andhra Pradesh, India. Known for its pristine beaches, scenic hills, and vibrant culture, it is a hub for tourism and industry.

The city offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and modernity, making it a must-visit destination.
